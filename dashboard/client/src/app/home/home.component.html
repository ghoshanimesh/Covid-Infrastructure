About US

We are a team of undergrads who have developed a project to track the infrastructure of one of the biggest pandemics - Covid-19 to help people with the knowledge of available beds at various hospitals with our app. It also predicts the number of cases that can possibly arrive the next day. It is a project built for our Final Year.

Awesome Features

Tracks Infrastructure

It is used to track the number of beds available at a particular hospital.

Cases Prediction

It helps to predict the number of cases that can arrive on the next day.

Symptoms & Prevention

Indicates the Symptoms and Prevention information on Covid-19.

Stats & Analysis

Gives an insight directly from the registered hospitals.
